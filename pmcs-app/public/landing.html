How PMCS Works

A structured four-stage digital chain of custody from depot dispatch to verified delivery — fully auditable and tamper-resistant.

01
Zone A
Dispatch & Loading

Tanker is registered, loaded, sealed, and a digital waybill is generated at the source depot. Volume and seal are recorded on-chain.

02
In Transit
Live Monitoring

GPS-enabled real-time tracking verifies the tanker follows its authorized route. Deviations and unauthorized stops trigger instant alerts.

03
Zone B
Checkpoint Handover

Zonal officers verify the tanker's seal integrity, manifested volume, and driver credentials at designated transfer checkpoints.

04
Zone C
Final Delivery

Destination depot scans and confirms final delivery volume. Any variance triggers an immediate compliance flag for regulatory review.

Platform Capabilities

Core Features

Six interconnected modules built for government-grade petroleum logistics oversight at national scale.

📡
GPS Live Tracking

Sub-60-second position updates for every active tanker. Geofenced corridors auto-detect route deviations and trigger zonal alerts in real time.

📄
Digital Waybill

QR-signed, tamper-evident electronic manifests replace paper documents. Every waybill is verifiable at any checkpoint along the route.

⚖️
Volume Verification

Automated comparison of loaded vs. delivered volumes with configurable tolerance thresholds. Variance reports are flagged for investigation.

🚨
Alerts Engine

Multi-tier alert system for route deviation, unauthorized stops, seal breach, and volume mismatch. Escalation paths route to the correct authority level.

🔗
Chain of Custody

Every custody transfer is digitally recorded with officer ID, timestamp, and GPS coordinates — creating an immutable audit trail from source to delivery.

📊
Reporting & Analytics

Regulatory dashboards, exportable compliance reports, zone-level performance metrics, and trend analytics for DPR and NNPC oversight.

Access Levels

Who Uses PMCS

Four purpose-designed user roles — each with scoped access, tailored views, and specific workflows.

🛡️
Super Admin

Full system access across all zones. Manages user accounts, system config, compliance thresholds, and regulatory exports for DPR and NNPC.

📍
Zonal Officer

Oversees fleet movements within an assigned zone. Verifies checkpoint handovers, investigates alerts, and approves transit clearances.

🏭
Depot Operator

Manages loading and receiving at a specific depot. Issues digital waybills, records volumes, verifies seals, and logs delivery confirmations.

🚛
Driver

Mobile-only access. Views assigned route, scans waypoint QR codes, receives instructions from zonal officers, and logs delivery confirmation.

Product Roadmap

Build Phases

A structured three-phase delivery from MVP pilot to a national intelligent petroleum monitoring network.

Phase 1 — MVP
Foundation
Months 1 – 4
  • GPS-enabled fleet tracking & geofencing
  • Digital waybill generation and QR verification
  • Driver mobile app (iOS & Android)
  • Depot loading and delivery confirmation module
  • Zonal officer dashboard and alert management
  • Basic compliance reports and audit logs
Phase 2 — Smart
Intelligence
Months 5 – 9
  • Volume sensor IoT integration at depots
  • AI-powered anomaly detection on routes
  • Predictive diversion risk scoring
  • Offline-first mobile sync architecture
  • Multi-zone dashboard for Super Admin
  • DPR regulatory data export & API gateway
Phase 3 — Intel
National Scale
Month 10+
  • Blockchain-anchored chain-of-custody ledger
